🚀 Key Innovations & Releases

coredns v1.14.2 lands with a powerful new proxyproto plugin. This is huge for preserving client IPs behind load balancers, giving you clearer visibility and control over your network traffic. No more guessing where that request truly originated!

Velero v1.18.0 introduces concurrent backup! Imagine orchestrating your data protection like a synchronized fleet, drastically reducing RTOs and RPOs. This is a game-changer for robust disaster recovery strategies and stateful applications.

External Secrets v2.1.0 brings vital security fixes and enhancements. In our security-first world, securely managing secrets is paramount. This release strengthens the bridge between external secret stores and Kubernetes, making your clusters safer and more compliant.

Inspektor Gadget v0.50.0 adds a trace_init_module gadget and image digest filtering. This is like equipping your observability toolkit with a microscopic lens and a high-precision filter, allowing you to peer deeper into kernel activities and ensure the integrity of your container images.
