🚀 Key Upgrades for Our Digital City

Cert-manager v1.20.0 brings alpha support for the new ListenerSet resource, alongside Azure Private DNS integration and Gateway API enhancements. This means our city's identity and access control system is becoming even more dynamic and integrated, streamlining how services prove who they are.

Harbor v2.15.0 introduces a crucial tag deletion option for garbage collection. Think of it as a much-needed spring cleaning for our central artifact repository, ensuring efficiency and reducing digital clutter.

Envoy & Istio have received multiple security fixes across various versions (e.g., Envoy v1.37.1, Istio 1.29.1). These patches are critical reinforcements for our service mesh, acting as the vigilant network police, protecting against vulnerabilities and keeping our city's traffic flowing securely.

Longhorn v1.11.1 delivers a significant memory leak fix for its instance manager. This is a vital stability improvement for our persistent storage, ensuring our digital city's foundations remain robust and reliable.
