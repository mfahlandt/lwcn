Last Week in Cloud Native (LWCN) is a weekly roundup of Cloud Native news from across the CNCF ecosystem. Every Monday we summarize the most important Kubernetes releases, CNCF project updates, security advisories (CVEs), and community news - written in a neutral, fact-first tone with no marketing hype. If you want to stay current on Cloud Native without reading dozens of changelogs and blogs, this is your weekly digest.

Coverage includes Kubernetes, Helm, Argo CD, Cilium, Prometheus, Envoy, Istio, Linkerd, Harbor, Flux, OpenTelemetry and 200+ other CNCF and Cloud Native projects. Browse the newsletter archive or read more about how we curate the news.
